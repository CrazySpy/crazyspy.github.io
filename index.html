About Me

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Renmin University of China, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaofeng Meng. Prior to this, I completed my M.Sc. degree at Yunnan University, where I was advised by Prof. Lizhen Wang.

My recent research focuses on Causal Inference and Spatio-Temporal Data Management. Currently, I am working on identifying invariants for traffic prediction tasks, such as traffic flow forecasting and travel time estimation. My goal is to design a robust model with strong generalization performance. I am also deeply interested in Large Language Models and hope to incorporate them into my future research.
