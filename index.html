About Me

I’m currently a first-year Ph.D. student at Renmin University, advised by Prof. Xiaofeng Meng.

My recent research centers on Domain Generalization. Broadly speaking, my research objective is to design a sufficiently robust method for identifying the invariant relationships between features and outcomes, thereby mitigating the impact of various environments.
